Cargo Handling Services

From the belly of the aircraft to the conveyor belt — baggage and freight, sorted, tracked, and transferred without the chaos.

The operation

Moving thousands of bags a day, calmly.

We staff the ramp, the sort room, and the transfer points so airlines and airports don't have to figure out who's holding the bag. We do.

  • Direct integration with WorldTracer, BagNet, and airline MCS
  • Containerized loading and unloading on every wide‑ and narrow‑body
  • Live sortation tracking with hourly throughput reporting
  • Insured, secure transfer for premium and high‑value freight
What we handle

Four ramp disciplines, one accountable team.

Loading & unloading

Belt loaders, container loaders, and ULD handling for narrow‑ and wide‑body fleets.

Sortation

Bag tag scanning, by‑destination sortation, and exception routing on a single dashboard.

Live tracking

RFID and barcode scans at every checkpoint, surfaced in your ops console in real time.

Secure transfer

High‑value, sensitive, and premium freight escorted with chain‑of‑custody logs.

For operators

From single‑flight contracts to full station ops.

Spinning up at a new station? Need extra hands during peak holiday windows? Our cargo team scales week‑by‑week so you only pay for the lift you actually need.

  • Per‑flight, per‑shift, or full‑station contracts
  • Peak‑season surge crews on 72 hours' notice
  • Weekly throughput and irregularity reports for ops leadership
FAQ

Good questions.

Which airports do you cover for cargo?

50+ U.S. stations with active cargo lift today, and we onboard a new station in roughly 30 days when an operator commits.

Do you handle dangerous goods?

Yes — our cargo teams are DG‑certified annually and we follow IATA DGR plus your airline's specific acceptance rules.

How is high‑value cargo tracked?

Two‑person handling, RFID seal, chain‑of‑custody log, and an opt‑in live GPS tag for the gate‑to‑truck leg. Signed acceptance on both ends.

What does it cost?

Per‑flight rates start from $185 per narrow‑body turn including standard ULD handling. Custom pricing for full‑station contracts.
