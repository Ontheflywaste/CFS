FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

How far in advance do I need to book?

Book up to 30 days ahead. Most same‑day requests (8+ hours before your flight) can still be fulfilled depending on availability.

Which airlines are supported?

All major U.S. carriers plus most international ones. If we can't tag with your airline at pickup, we'll transparently say so during booking.

What if my flight changes last minute?

Let us know via the app. If we haven't dropped yet, we'll re‑route to the new flight. If we have, we'll coordinate retrieval.

Are there weight or size limits?

We follow your airline's checked‑bag rules. Oversize items (skis, golf clubs) are supported — just flag them at booking.
