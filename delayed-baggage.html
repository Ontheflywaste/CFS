Delayed Baggage Recovery

When a bag misses its flight, what happens next is what passengers actually remember. We turn baggage irregularity into a moment of service — delivered fast, tracked end‑to‑end, and signed off cleanly.

Overview

Built to find, verify, and return every bag.

Our recovery network plugs directly into carrier systems (WorldTracer, BagNet, MCS). The moment a bag is matched, the nearest Casual Flight Services agent is dispatched — no manual handoff, no phone tag.

  • Average delivery within 4 hours of bag arrival at destination
  • 24/7 dispatch across 50+ U.S. airports
  • Signed proof of delivery, photo confirmation, GPS trail
  • Live passenger SMS updates, branded to your airline
How recovery works

Four steps from mis‑routed bag to reunited passenger.

Ticket opened

Your airline's MCS or a passenger report triggers a Casual Flight Services recovery ticket automatically.

Agent assigned

The nearest available agent is routed with ETA and delivery address in their app.

Bag retrieved

Agent picks up the bag at the carousel, verifies the tag, and photographs it.

Delivered

Dropped off with signature + photo. The ticket closes in your system in real time.

FAQ

Common questions.

How fast do you typically deliver a delayed bag?

Median is under 4 hours from bag arrival at the destination airport to handoff at the passenger's door. Urban routes are faster; remote hotels can run longer.

Do you integrate with our existing baggage system?

Yes — we integrate with WorldTracer, BagNet, and most carrier MCS platforms. Implementation is usually one sprint for a new station.

What happens if a passenger isn't home?

Agents hold for 10 minutes, call, and either leave the bag at a pre‑approved concierge/location or reschedule. Everything is logged with photos.

Is there coverage outside my hub airport?

We operate in 50+ U.S. airports with more added every quarter. Beyond our footprint we can broker with vetted regional partners under the same SLA.
