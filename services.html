Our services

Everything between the curb and the claim belt.

Four focused service lines, each built around a single promise: the smoothest possible handoff between you, your passengers, and their bags.

Service 01

Delayed Baggage Recovery

When a bag misses its flight, what happens next determines whether a passenger becomes a lifelong customer — or a complaint in your inbox. CFS turns baggage irregularity into a moment of service.

  • Direct integration with WorldTracer, BagNet, and carrier MCS
  • Average delivery within 4 hours of bag arrival at destination
  • Live passenger SMS updates, branded to your airline
  • Signed proof of delivery and photo confirmation every time
Service 02

Home Bag Pickup & Check‑In

We show up at your door the day of — or the evening before — your flight, weigh and tag your bags, and deliver them straight to baggage claim at your destination.

  • 2‑hour arrival window of your choice
  • Your airline's tags applied on‑site before we leave your door
  • End‑to‑end GPS tracking; you watch every handoff
  • Standard insurance up to $2,500 per bag, extendable
Service 03

Wheelchair & Mobility Assistance

Our attendants are trained, background‑checked, and — most importantly — patient. We treat every passenger like we'd treat our own grandparents, because many of them are.

  • Curb‑to‑gate, gate‑to‑gate, and gate‑to‑curb coverage
  • ACAA‑compliant training and annual recertification
  • Electric and standard wheelchair fleet, maintained daily
  • Dedicated family and companion coordination
Service 04

Airport Check‑In Services

Meet us at the curb. We'll handle your airline check‑in, print boarding passes, tag your bags, and walk you to the security line. Especially useful for families, groups, and premium travelers.

  • Curb‑side greet at a pre‑booked time
  • Full check‑in, bag drop, and boarding pass delivery
  • Optional TSA/PreCheck line escort
  • Group and corporate travel plans available
