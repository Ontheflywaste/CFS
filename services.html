Our services

Everything between the curb and the claim belt.

Seven focused service lines, each built around a single promise: the smoothest possible handoff between you, your passengers, and their bags.

Service 01

Delayed Baggage Recovery

When a bag misses its flight, what happens next determines whether a passenger becomes a lifelong customer — or a complaint in your inbox. Casual Flight Services turns baggage irregularity into a moment of service.

  • Direct integration with WorldTracer, BagNet, and carrier MCS
  • Average delivery within 4 hours of bag arrival at destination
  • Live passenger SMS updates, branded to your airline
  • Signed proof of delivery and photo confirmation every time
See full details
Service 02

Home Bag Pickup & Check‑In

We show up at your door the day of — or the evening before — your flight, weigh and tag your bags, and deliver them straight to baggage claim at your destination.

  • 2‑hour arrival window of your choice
  • Your airline's tags applied on‑site before we leave your door
  • End‑to‑end GPS tracking; you watch every handoff
  • Standard insurance up to $2,500 per bag, extendable
See full details
Service 03

Wheelchair & Mobility Assistance

Our attendants are trained, background‑checked, and — most importantly — patient. We treat every passenger like we'd treat our own grandparents, because many of them are.

  • Curb‑to‑gate, gate‑to‑gate, and gate‑to‑curb coverage
  • ACAA‑compliant training and annual recertification
  • Electric and standard wheelchair fleet, maintained daily
  • Dedicated family and companion coordination
See full details
Service 04

Airport Check‑In Services

Meet us at the curb. We'll handle your airline check‑in, print boarding passes, tag your bags, and walk you to the security line. Especially useful for families, groups, and premium travelers.

  • Curb‑side greet at a pre‑booked time
  • Full check‑in, bag drop, and boarding pass delivery
  • Optional TSA/PreCheck line escort
  • Group and corporate travel plans available
See full details
Service 05

Cargo Handling

Baggage and freight, sorted, tracked, and transferred between aircraft and terminal — without the chaos. We staff the ramp, the sort room, and the transfer points so airlines and airports don't have to figure out who's holding the bag.

  • Containerized loading and unloading on every wide‑ and narrow‑body
  • Live sortation tracking with hourly throughput reporting
  • Insured, secure transfer for premium and high‑value freight
  • Per‑flight, per‑shift, or full‑station contracts
See full details
Service 06

Ground Handling

Aircraft ground support between flights — marshalling, towing, fueling coordination, cabin turn, GPU connection, and pre‑departure checks. The ramp work that keeps your schedule honest.

  • Certified marshallers and tug operators on every shift
  • Cabin cleaning and cabin reset within posted turn times
  • Fueling coordination with your contracted fuel vendor
  • GPU connection, pre‑departure checks, and push‑back
See full details
Service 07

Security Services

Access control, passenger and baggage screening support, secure escorts, and perimeter monitoring — TSA‑aware and airport‑authority compliant. Our officers move with the calm of someone who's seen the bad days.

  • Access control at restricted gates, jet bridges, and SIDA zones
  • Screening support for passenger and baggage lanes
  • Secure escort for high‑value, sensitive, or VIP movements
  • 24/7 perimeter monitoring with documented incident logs
