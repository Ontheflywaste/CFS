Airport Check‑In Services

Pull up, step out, walk to security. Our agents handle your airline check‑in, bag drop, and boarding passes on your behalf — right at the curb.

The experience

Skip the counter. Keep your time.

For families with young kids, groups traveling together, premium passengers, or anyone who values 30 minutes back — we take the part of travel day that wastes time and make it disappear.

  • Curb‑side greet at a pre‑booked time
  • Full check‑in, bag drop, and boarding pass handoff
  • Optional TSA / PreCheck line escort
  • Group and corporate travel plans available

FAQ

Good questions.

Which airports offer this service?

50+ U.S. airports and growing. Check availability by ZIP code at booking; we transparently show which airlines + terminals we cover.

How early do I need to arrive?

Arrive 60 minutes before domestic and 2 hours before international. We do the counter work while you wait curbside.

Can this be combined with wheelchair assistance?

Yes — pair Check‑In with Wheelchair Assistance and we'll take the handoff all the way to your gate, on the same ticket.

What does it cost?

From $39 per traveler for standard check‑in. Group rates for 10+ and corporate accounts available on request.
