What we do

Seven services. One calm, end‑to‑end experience.

Whether you're an airline reuniting a passenger with a missing suitcase, an airport partner staffing your ramp, or a traveler who just wants to skip the counter — Casual Flight Services is built to make the hard part invisible.

Delayed Baggage Recovery

When a bag misses its flight, we find it, track it, and deliver it to the passenger — at home, hotel, or office — with full visibility for your ops team.

Home Bag Pickup
