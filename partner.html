Partner With Us

A ground services partner your operation can hand the hard stuff to.

For airports, airlines, FBOs, and ground handlers — Casual Flight Services plugs into your workflow as a vendor and takes baggage irregularity, mobility assistance, and curbside check‑in off your plate.

Why partners choose us

Operational reliability without the headcount.

We sit between you and the passenger, absorbing the tasks that don't fit cleanly inside an airline or airport's existing org chart. Our dispatch network, agents, and tooling are built for the messy 5% — the bags that miss the connection, the wheelchair that didn't show up, the curbside passenger with three suitcases and a tight flight.

Every engagement is governed by a clear SLA, transparent reporting, and direct integration with the systems you already run.

What we handle

Four service lines you can adopt in any combination.

Delayed baggage recovery

WorldTracer / BagNet / MCS integration, dispatched recovery agents, real‑time status. We get bags to passengers within hours, not days.

Home‑to‑airport pickup

Curbside collection, airline check‑in, tag‑and‑deliver. Offer it as an airline amenity, hotel concierge program, or standalone service.

Wheelchair & mobility assistance

ACAA‑compliant curb‑to‑gate, gate‑to‑gate, and unaccompanied minor programs. Calm, safe, always documented.

Curbside & remote check‑in

Off‑airport check‑in for cruise terminals, hotels, and corporate offices — skip the counter, board faster.

How a partnership works

Four steps from first call to live coverage.

1 · Discovery

We review your routes, irregularity volume, and existing handler footprint. Usually one 30‑minute call.

2 · Scoping

You receive a written SOW: service lines, station list, SLAs, reporting cadence, and pricing — all in one document.

3 · Integration

System hook‑ups (WorldTracer / MCS / your CRM), agent onboarding at the chosen stations, and a 2‑week ramp.

4 · Live coverage

We start taking tickets. You get a named ops lead, a Slack/Teams channel, and weekly performance reporting.
