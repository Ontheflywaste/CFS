Security Services

Access control, passenger and baggage screening support, secure escorts, and perimeter monitoring — TSA‑aware and airport‑authority compliant.

The team

Security that reads the room.

Trained for the cadence of a working airport. Our officers move with the calm of someone who's seen the bad days and run the playbook.

  • Access control at restricted gates, jet bridges, and SIDA zones
  • Screening support for passenger and baggage lanes
  • Secure escort for high‑value, sensitive, or VIP movements
  • 24/7 perimeter monitoring with documented incident logs
What we handle

Four layers, one unified post.

Access control

Badge checks, manifest verification, and SIDA gate posts staffed around the clock.

Screening support

Lane staffing, divest support, and secondary‑screening coordination with TSA.

Secure escort

Two‑officer details for VIP, high‑value cargo, and sensitive personnel movements.

Perimeter monitoring

CCTV review, fence‑line patrols, and incident logging with airport authority handoff.

Compliance

Compliant with TSA, the airport authority, and your auditor.

Every post is documented. Every incident has a timestamp. Every officer has a current background check on file — and we share the paperwork before you ever ask.

  • TSA‑cleared officers; STA on file for every badge
  • Quarterly recurrent training and incident response drills
  • Audit‑ready logs with retention aligned to local authority policy
FAQ

Good questions.

Are your officers TSA‑cleared?

Yes — every officer carries a current STA, and we maintain a posting roster the airport authority can audit at any time.

Do you support SIDA badge requirements?

We post officers at SIDA gates and jet‑bridge doors with badge verification, manifest cross‑check, and live escort for non‑badged personnel.

How do you handle incident reporting?

Every incident gets a timestamped digital log within 30 minutes, copy to the airport authority, and a 24‑hour follow‑up report.

What's the response time on an alert?

Under 90 seconds at staffed posts. Mobile response teams average under 4 minutes anywhere in the secured perimeter.
