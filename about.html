About Casual Flight Services

A ground services company built for the way people travel today.

We started Casual Flight Services after one too many stories of lost bags, missed connections, and elderly travelers left waiting at empty gates. Aviation deserves a ground partner that treats every bag and every passenger like they matter — because they do.

Our mission

Make the hard parts of air travel feel invisible.

Airports are stressful enough. Whether a bag was rerouted, a wheelchair is needed, or you just want to hand your suitcase off at the door — Casual Flight Services is the quiet team in the background making sure it all just works.

What we believe

Three values we refuse to compromise on.

Quiet excellence

The best service is the one you barely notice. We show up uniformed, on time, and trained — every single shift.

Radical transparency

Every handoff is timestamped. Every passenger gets updates. Every partner gets a dashboard. No surprises — ever.

People first

Bags are bags. The people receiving them — passengers, ops managers, gate agents — are what actually matter.
